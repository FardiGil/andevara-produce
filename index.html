About Andevara

Export experience shaped into a focused produce brokerage model.

Andevara Produce brings together commercial understanding, sourcing visibility, and practical coordination for international buyers seeking premium produce from Peru.

Fardi Gil is a Peruvian produce export professional with more than 12 years of experience working with mango, avocado, organic ginger, and organic turmeric for international markets including North America, Europe, and Asia.

Through Andevara Produce, he now focuses on connecting trusted Peruvian supply with importers seeking reliability, transparency, and long-term commercial relationships.

  • Hands-on understanding of sourcing, packing, cold chain, and export coordination.
  • Commercial perspective shaped by years of working directly with international buyers.
  • Clear focus on building dependable B2B relationships with commitment to traceability.
